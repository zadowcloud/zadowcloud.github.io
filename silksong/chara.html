Meet the Characters

Hornet

Hornet is a skilled warrior and the protagonist of Silksong. She wields a needle and thread as her primary weapons and embarks on a journey to unravel the mysteries of the kingdom. She possesses incredible agility and acrobatic skills, allowing her to traverse challenging terrains and engage in fast-paced combat.

Lace

Lace is a mysterious figure in Silksong. Not much is known about Lace, but they possess powerful magical abilities. They play a significant role in the story, often assisting Hornet in her quest. Lace's motives and origins are shrouded in secrecy, adding an air of intrigue to their character.

Sherma

Sherma is a loyal companion to Hornet throughout her journey in Silksong. They are known for their exceptional agility and nimbleness. Sherma's role is to provide valuable support to Hornet in battles and help her navigate challenging environments. Sherma shares a deep bond with Hornet and their presence offers both assistance and emotional support to the protagonist.
